General Information

Robotic vacuum cleaner, or a "robovac", is a vacuum cleaner that has a certain level of autonomy to clean while some have additional methods like spinning brushes, mopping or UV sterilization.

Some of the earliest home vacuum cleaners needed two people to operate them - one to vacuum and the other to push or pull the heavy machinery.

Some of the vacuum cleaners even have sensors that tell when the surface has been sufficiently cleaned.

Fun Fact: Rambo Amadeus, a Belgrade-based Montenegrin singer-songwriter, has a practice to sometimes make performances which include playing on household appliances among which are predominant vacuum cleaners.

Some Facts

The first ever robotic vacuum cleaner appeared in 1996.

The company Dyson built and demonstrated a robot vacuum in 2001, but because of its high price, it was never released to the market.
