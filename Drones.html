General Information

Drones are aerial vehicles that do not need piloting.

Military or governmental organizations use them in less accessible areas, such as war zones.

Drones are mostly used for photography.

Farmers often use drones to check on the condition of their crops and help determine which areas are ready for harvest.

Some Facts

The first military drone, the Predator, targeted Osama bin Laden.

The first real drone was made in 2002

The market size for commercial drones in 2016 was $2.8 billion.
