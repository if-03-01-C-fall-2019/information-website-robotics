General Information

Two belgian hospitals already have robots called "Pepper" working as receptionists.

In Japan there is a robot called the "Robear", he is shaped as a giant, gentle bear with a cartoonish head. He can lift and move patients in and out of bed into a wheelchair, help patients to stand, and to turn them to prevent bed sores as many times as you want. The Robear is there to save human personal from having to carry out strenous tasks, such as lifting patients out of bed.

One of the really well developed medical robots is the daVinci robot. He is so precise that he can stitch a grape together.

Some Facts

Medical Robots are used to help the human personal in hospitals

Some robots are also just there to relivie stress and calm patients down they are called Companion bots
