General Information

A robot has been created that uses bacteria-filled fuel cells. The robot is capable of producing electricity from dead flies to power its battery. It can also use rotten apples to create fuel.

Robot comes from the Chzech word "robota" which means "forced work or labor".

The smallest robot is called a nanobot. It is less than one-thousandth of a millimeter.

The first humanoid robot was debuted in 1939. Elektro, built by Westinghouse, was seven feet tall and could 'speak' 700 words.

The number of robots in use at the moment is more than a million. And over half of these robots are based in Japan, the epicentre of technology.

What Robots do

Robots are often used to perform jobs that are too risky and dangerous for.

The annual global sales value in 2018 was $16.5 billion.

422,000 robots were shipped globally in 2018.
