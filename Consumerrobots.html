Consumer Robots

People in the 1900 thought that personal robots like swiping robots,grooming robots and so on would be common by now.

Some of the first home robots were toys. Already in the 1980s was a programmable robot one of the hottest gifts.

Robots are officially genderless, but service robots are more likely to be female, because they usually fulfill traditionally feminine duties like caretaking.

The future home robots will be able to recognize whom they are serving, with the help of facial recognition.
